About Jovanna Penn-Reina

Hello my name is Jovanna Penn-Reina. I am a 18 year old, first year Creative Media Production at Massey University Wellington.

I am from Whanganui, New Zealand. I love photography and filmmaking. My favourite kind of photography is landscape photography. I don't really enjoy working with models that often. I love capturing the nature and landscpaes around me. Living in New Zealand gives me a great opportunity to do this because New Zealand has beautiful landscapes everywhere.
